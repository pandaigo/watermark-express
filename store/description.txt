==============================
ストア説明文（Description）
==============================

Add text & logo watermarks to your images instantly. Perfect for e-commerce sellers, content creators, and photographers who need to protect their product photos.

Features:
- Text watermarks with custom font, color, size, and opacity
- Logo watermarks (PNG) for brand consistency (Pro)
- 9 position options + diagonal tiled repeat
- Real-time preview before downloading
- Bulk processing — watermark multiple images at once
- Download all as ZIP (Pro)
- Save watermark templates for reuse (Pro)
- JPEG quality control
- 100% local — your images never leave your device

Free: 10 images/day, text watermark only
Pro ($7.99 one-time): Unlimited images, logo watermarks, ZIP download, templates

==============================
単一用途の説明
==============================
This extension has a single purpose: to add text or logo watermarks to images locally in the browser, with no external server processing.

==============================
storage が必要な理由
==============================
Required to save the user's watermark settings, templates, logo image, and payment status locally on the device using chrome.storage.local. No data is sent to any external server.

==============================
ホスト権限が必要な理由（content_scripts の matches）
==============================
https://extensionpay.com/*:
Required by ExtensionPay (the payment provider) to detect when a payment is completed. The ExtPay.js content script runs on the ExtensionPay checkout page to communicate payment status back to the extension.

==============================
リモートコードを使用しているか
==============================
No. This extension does not use any remotely hosted code. All scripts are bundled within the extension package.
